Welcome to Group 12's Human Machine Interface (HMI) Website

The Human Machine Interaction (HMI) course focuses on studying and developing interactions between humans and machines, enhancing user experience and optimizing performance. Our team, Group 12, is dedicated to exploring design principles, analysis, and real-world applications, aiming to deliver innovative and efficient solutions.

courses materials

Week 1: Intro and Overview

In Week 1, we will introduce the fundamentals of Human Machine Interface (HMI) and provide an overview of the course structure. We will explore key concepts such as usability, interaction design, and user experience (UX). This session will set the foundation for understanding the relationship between humans and machines, and how effective interfaces can enhance this interaction.

Duration: 1 Week

Class Time: 12.30pm - 3.05pm

Topics Covered: Course Overview, Key HMI Concepts, Usability, User-Centered Design

Week 2: Conceptualizing Interaction

In Week 2 of our Human Machine Interaction (HMI) course, we delve into the fundamental principles of conceptualizing interaction between users and machines. This week focuses on understanding how users perceive and engage with technology, emphasizing the design and evaluation of effective user interfaces.

Duration: 1 Week

Class Time: 12.30pm - 3.05pm

Topics Covered: Course Overview, Key HMI Concepts, Usability, User-Centered Design
